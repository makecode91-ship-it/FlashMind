How FlashMind uses AI

FlashMind is built around practical AI use cases for education: helping students create better study materials, understand difficult topics, and study consistently with personalized support.

Generate study materials: quizzes, flashcards, summaries, notes, courses, and explanations.
Personalized learning: adapt explanations and practice sessions to the user’s level, language, and weak areas.
Conversational study assistant: help students ask questions, explore topics, and review concepts through AI chat and voice learning.
